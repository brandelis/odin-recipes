Old fashioned

Description

Pick up your pipe, grow out your 'stache, and show off your manliness with this classic whiskey drink.

This is a simple mixer where the quality of your ingredients matters.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon or rye whiskey
  • 1/4 oz simple syrup
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Orange peel or Luxardo cherry, for garnish
  • 1 large ice cube

Steps

  1. Mix the syrup and bitters with your ice. Stir to combine.
  2. Add your whiskey and stir again.
  3. Run a slice of orange peel around the rim of the glass then add as a garnish.
  4. Drink while holding the finger of one hand across your chest and under your blazer. Like a proper classic gent.
  5. Be careful that your 'stache doesn't grow out too quickly.
  6. Beat the sexual attention from others away with a stick.
