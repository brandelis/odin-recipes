Sex on the beach

Description

A refreshing cocktail combining vodka, peach schnapps, orange juice, and cranberry juice. This vibrant drink offers a perfect balance of sweet and tangy flavours, garnished with an orange slice and served over crushed ice.

Ideal for Summer gatherings, this tropical classic evokes seaside relaxation with every sip.

Ingredients

  • 50ml Vodka
  • 25ml Peach Schnapps
  • The juice of 2 Oranges
  • 50ml Cranberry juice
  • Crushed ice

Steps

  1. Fill a jug with crushed ice.
  2. Add the liquids and mix.
  3. Serve in tall glasses.
